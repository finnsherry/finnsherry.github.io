Eikonal PDE

In "Crossing-Preserving Geodesic Tracking on Spherical Images", we model blood vessels as geodesics of Riemannian distances on position-orientation spaces. It turns out that these distances are the (viscosity) solutions of corresponding eikonal PDEs. Here I go over how we can solve the eikonal PDE practically to recover the distance map. I also wrote some shaders so we can see it in practice.

Flow Matching on Lie Groups

In "Flow Matching on Lie Groups", we developed a new technique for generative modelling on Lie groups, generalising Flow Matching by Lipman et al. As the name implies, this works by flowing data from an initial to a target distribution. Here are some nice videos of this in action.
